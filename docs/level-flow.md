# Level Flow

## Setting

The campaign should unfold through an underground research facility. Each level represents another layer of containment between Mike and freedom.

This setting supports several useful design goals:

- Industrial architecture creates clear puzzle spaces, chokepoints, vents, and hazardous machinery.
- Scientists, guards, and automated defenses can all act as believable sources of danger.
- The facility can escalate naturally from simple test chambers to heavily secured escape routes.

## Level Structure

Each level should function as a contained puzzle space with platforming pressure.

Recommended structure:

1. Introduce a traversal restriction or hazard.
2. Teach how that danger can alter Mike's size, state, or pathing.
3. Ask the player to use the resulting split bodies intelligently.
4. End at a Recombobulator that converts survival and positioning into level completion.

## Hazards And Opposition

Mike will encounter both passive hazards and active enemies.

- Hazards reshape the puzzle space by changing Mike's condition or body count.
- Minions and guards exist to pressure movement, timing, and positioning.
- Enemies are obstacles first and combat targets second.

Mike should rarely attack directly. Instead, he creates outcomes indirectly by manipulating the map and its hazards, sometimes causing collateral damage to whoever is trying to stop him.

## Recombobulator Goal

At the end of each level there is a Recombobulator.

Its role in the design:

- It is the extraction point for Mike's scattered mass.
- It turns multi-body chaos back into a clean victory condition.
- It rewards efficient routing and smart sacrifice.

Rules for the concept:

- The machine requires a target percentage of Mike's total mass.
- When a Mike piece reaches the Recombobulator, that piece is locked in place and can no longer move.
- Once the required mass threshold is met, all remaining Mike pieces are summoned into the machine.
- Mike is restored to his original full size before the next level begins.

## Progression Ideas

- Early levels focus on learning that damage is useful.
- Mid-game levels combine multiple hazards so the player must choose which kind of split or state change is desirable.
- Later levels can demand routing several Mike bodies through different constraints before cashing them out at the Recombobulator.

The overall campaign fantasy is not just escape. It is learning how to operate an impossible body inside a system built to destroy it.
