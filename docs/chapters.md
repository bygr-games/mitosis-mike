# Chapters

## Recommended Campaign Size

Mitosis Mike should have five chapters.

That structure is large enough to fully develop the core mitosis mechanic, introduce new hazards in layers, and create a satisfying escape arc without pushing the game into content bloat. It also fits the tone of a contained laboratory breakout: each chapter feels like a deeper and more hostile part of the facility.

## Chapter 1: Containment

### Theme

Clinical test rooms, observation glass, holding cells, security checkpoints, and simple containment devices.

### Purpose

This chapter teaches the game's core inversion: taking damage is not failure. The player learns that Mike's body changes under pressure and that hazards can be useful.

### Mechanical Focus

- Basic movement and puzzle-platform controls.
- First exposure to splitting.
- Small passages that reward becoming smaller.
- Introductory enemies and hazards with readable timing.

### Narrative Beat

Mike escapes the area where he was studied and begins to understand what his body can do.

## Chapter 2: Waste Processing

### Theme

Pipes, drains, incinerators, disposal chutes, runoff channels, and filthy industrial machinery.

### Purpose

This chapter reframes hazards as tools. The player should stop thinking only about avoiding danger and start thinking about how to use it deliberately.

### Mechanical Focus

- Environmental timing puzzles.
- Stronger emphasis on routing Mike through hazardous spaces.
- Early combinations of split-state puzzles and traversal pressure.
- Natural home for future fire and water mechanics.

### Narrative Beat

Mike moves from the sterile experiment wing into the ugly machinery that processes the lab's mistakes.

## Chapter 3: Chemical Labs

### Theme

Potion vats, unstable compounds, mutation chambers, specimen tanks, and sabotaged control rooms.

### Purpose

This chapter expands the design beyond raw mitosis. It is the right place to introduce status-altering mechanics and experiments that change how Mike behaves.

### Mechanical Focus

- Growth potion puzzles.
- Confusion or inverted-control sequences.
- Multi-step rooms where the player must prepare Mike's state before attempting the route.
- Denser interactions between enemies, hazards, and consumables.

### Narrative Beat

Mike enters the part of the facility that explains why he exists at all: the labs that engineered him.

## Chapter 4: Security Block

### Theme

Turrets, patrols, bulkhead doors, alarms, kill corridors, and hardened escape-prevention systems.

### Purpose

This chapter increases pressure. The player should already understand the core mechanics, so the game can now demand faster decisions, cleaner routing, and more intentional use of multiple Mike bodies.

### Mechanical Focus

- Enemy-driven timing puzzles.
- Rooms that require managing several Mike bodies at once.
- Repeated tradeoffs between keeping mass safe and spending mass to progress.
- More hostile combinations of mobility constraints and ranged threats.

### Narrative Beat

The facility is no longer reacting passively. It is actively trying to contain Mike before he reaches the exit.

## Chapter 5: Recombobulator Core

### Theme

The deepest central machinery of the complex: power conduits, mass-routing systems, unstable reactors, and the final recombination apparatus.

### Purpose

This is the payoff chapter. It should bring together every lesson the player has learned about sacrifice, splitting, state changes, hazards, and reconstruction.

### Mechanical Focus

- Large final rooms that test the full move set.
- Multi-route puzzles where different Mike bodies solve different constraints.
- Endgame use of the Recombobulator as more than a doorway, turning it into the centerpiece of the final puzzle logic.
- A closing sequence where restoration and escape happen together.

### Narrative Beat

Mike reaches the heart of the system that was built to control bodies like his, then uses it to reclaim himself and break out.

## Arc Summary

The five-chapter progression should feel like this:

- Chapter 1: Discovery.
- Chapter 2: Adaptation.
- Chapter 3: Mutation.
- Chapter 4: Resistance.
- Chapter 5: Escape.

This gives the game a clear emotional and mechanical arc. Mike starts as a victim of the lab, learns to exploit its dangers, uncovers the experimental logic behind his condition, survives the facility's direct response, and finally escapes by mastering the same systems meant to imprison him.
